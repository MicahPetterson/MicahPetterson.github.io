A Timeless Pasttime

My personal favorite hobby that is completely real and not fictional is venturing out into the vast and expansive network of alternate timelines. I have been doing this for only about a year now, but I can truthfully say that it's a thrill every single time to set foot into a completely different iteration of reality as we know it. The only downside is that I can't really make this knowledge public, or world governments would be all over me. It's a good thing I'm not putting all of this information on some publicy accessible website or anything.

Oh yeah, that orange crayon symbol appears in all of my photos and I'm not sure why. It's not like these photos were generated by the Craiyon AI image generation algorithm and I'm using this paragraph to give proper credit to the source for my images. That would be ridiculous.

About

My name is Micah Petterson, and I'm a timeline-hopping enthusiast. The interest started when I was walking home from school one day. I must've been pretty worn out from the day's classes, because I forgot to properly align my axes with the dimensional plane and ended up slipping between realities. It took me a few millenia and the obtainment of immortality to get back to my home timeline, but once I did, I realized the whole experience had been kind of fun! So I decided to make a hobby out of it.

Highlights

Taking the time to plan out a proper exploration of a timeline is a lot of work, so I haven't gotten a good look at much more than a couple dozen realities. Even so, I've seen some pretty fascinating stuff, and would like to share a few of the more noteable discoveries here.

Timeline 43ARC-6

One of my first expeditions! This timeline must have been abandoned a long time ago. Despite the medieval architecture, there wasn't a soul to be found. I didn't stay very long, as I started to feel like something was watching me. Haven't gone back to that one since!

Timeline θREiD-IF

As breathtaking as the sunset was in this timeline, the air was hardly breathable. I was only able to stay for a few minutes. I'm pretty sure what you're seeing in the sky there are rings! Yeah, rings around the Earth! I have no clue how that came to be in this reality, but it looks pretty amazing.

Timeline ΩRIGIN

I...can't remember much about my time in this timeline. There was this city, but it wasn't inhabited by humans. I don't know how I actually got to this timeline in the first place. I can't access it with my current equipment, so...how did I get there?

My Equipment

Using an old toaster, a pizza box, approximately seven paperclips, and a couple other things I had lying around, I was able to slap together my own interdimensional transporter. Naturally it's nowhere near as good as the professionals' equipment, but still nothing to scoff at!

I just finished recalibrating it shortly before typing this out, actually. So I should be good to take another ride soon without worrying about getting stuck in some weird limbo dimension. That's happened more times than I care to remember.
